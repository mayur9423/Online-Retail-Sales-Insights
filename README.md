# Overall Conclusion

This business has shown strong overall performance with **$10.6M revenue**, **19,960 invoices**, and a presence across **38 countries**.

Sales peak strongly during the **September–November** period, indicating high seasonal demand.

**November** is the peak month, while **December** shows a sharp decline. However, before concluding that the business declined, we should first verify whether the December data is complete. A low final month can occur when the dataset ends mid-month.

The total revenue generated is **$10.6M**, and the monthly sales trend explains how this revenue was built over time.

## Key Business Insight

Revenue is highly dependent on the **UK market**, which creates a concentration risk for the business.

## Recommendations

- Expand and strengthen international markets to reduce dependency on a single region.
- Diversify the product catalog to improve revenue stability.
- Improve inventory planning based on seasonal demand trends, especially during the September–November peak period.
