# Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel Project)

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end retail sales analysis built entirely in Microsoft Excel using an online retail dataset. The objective of the project was to clean raw transactional data, perform exploratory analysis, build interactive dashboards, and generate business insights that support decision-making.

The project demonstrates practical data analytics skills using Excel tools such as Power Query, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Timelines, Dashboard Design, and Business Insight Reporting.

---

## Business Objective

The goal of this project was to analyze retail transaction data to:

* Understand overall sales performance
* Identify high-performing products and countries
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Discover seasonal sales trends
* Build an interactive dashboard for quick business reporting
* Generate actionable business insights and recommendations

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains online retail transaction records including:

* Invoice information
* Product descriptions
* Quantities sold
* Unit prices
* Customer IDs
* Countries
* Transaction dates

The data was cleaned and transformed before analysis to improve consistency and reporting quality.

### Key Metrics

| Metric                           | Value                                                                |

| Total Invoices                   | 19,960                                                               |

| Countries Served                 | 38                                                                   |

| International Sales Contribution | 1–2%                                                                 |
| Peak Sales Period                | September to November                                                |
| Top Revenue Products             | DOTCOM POSTAGE, REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER, PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE |

> Note: December 2011 data may be incomplete. Any visible sales decline during this period should be interpreted cautiously.

## Key Insights

### 1. Strong Revenue Performance

The business generated approximately **$10.6M** in total revenue across nearly **20,000 invoices**, indicating a high transaction volume.

### 2. Sales Concentration

A small group of products contributed a significant portion of total revenue:

* DOTCOM POSTAGE
* REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
* PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE

Together, these products contributed approximately **47% of total sales revenue**.

### 3. Seasonal Sales Trends

Sales activity peaked between **September and November**, suggesting strong seasonal demand during this period.

### 4. Geographic Distribution

Sales were highly concentrated in domestic markets, contributing approximately **98%** of total revenue, while international markets represented a relatively small share.

### 5. Customer Purchase Behavior

The average invoice value of **$533.17** suggests relatively high-value transactions across customers.

## Business Recommendations

### Expand High-Performing Product Categories

Focus inventory planning and marketing efforts on the top-performing products that contribute the highest share of revenue.

### Improve International Market Reach

Since international sales contribution is relatively low, the business may benefit from:

* Expanding shipping options
* Localized promotions
* Country-specific marketing campaigns

### Prepare for Seasonal Demand

Increase inventory and promotional campaigns during the September–November peak sales period to maximize revenue opportunities.

### Monitor Product Dependency

Because a limited number of products contribute a large share of revenue, diversifying product performance may reduce business risk.
